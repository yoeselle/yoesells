About me

I'm Yoesel, a first-year Computer Science student at Curtin, a content strategist, and a filmmaker. I've got a foot in both the technical and creative worlds, and that's honestly my favourite part: I get to solve problems from both sides.

On the technical side I'm still early in my coding journey, but problem-solving is where I'm strongest. I break things down quickly, find the angle nobody's looked at yet, and figure out how to ship. Comfortable with logic, systems thinking, and using AI tools to move faster on whatever I'm building.

I've been making content since I was ten. YouTube vlogs, travel TikToks, late-night edits that definitely went past bedtime. What started as a hobby became a way of thinking. Now I help brands show up online with intentional, strategic content that actually means something to the people watching.

I work best when the people I'm working with feel comfortable. No jargon, no mystery, no gatekeeping, just honest conversations, clear thinking, and work I'm proud to put my name on. Whether you're a founder, a team, or a fellow creative, I'd love to hear what you're building.
